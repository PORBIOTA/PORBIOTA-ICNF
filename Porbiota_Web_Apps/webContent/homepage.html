Select the app you wish to use:

Species Extractor - Creates a .CSV with the results obtained by cross referencing the species name in one .CSV with those from GBIF API.

Location Extractor - Creates a .CSV with the results obtained by inputing WGS84 coordinates into the OpenStreetMaps API.

Metadata Extractor- Creates a .TXT with the metadata info extracted from a .CSV darwinCore-like file.

Time Zone Extractor- Creates a .CSV containing the Time Zone for the decimal coordinates.

MGRS to Lat Long Converter - Creates a .CSV by converting MGRS coordinates to decimal Longitude and Latitude.

Initial Column Adder - Creates a UTF-8 .CSV adding the initial GBIF fixed columns.

Table Joiner - Creates a UTF-8 .CSV by merging two or more .CSVs together.


OCR - Creates a .TXT or .PDF by performing OCR on multiple images or a .PDF.

OCR for Table - Creates a .CSV by performing OCR on an image.

.Txt Tagger - Tags a .txt for species, locations, dates and others.

Tag to Csv - Converts a tagged .txt to a .csv.
